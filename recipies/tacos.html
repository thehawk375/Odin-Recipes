Tacos

Home made tacos

Ingredients

  • ground beef
  • onion
  • jalepeno (optional
  • taco shells, soft or hard

Steps

  1. dice onion and jalepeno
  2. brown and season ground beef and sautee onion, mix it together
  3. add a pack of taco seasoning to the vegetable / beef mix
  4. simmer for 5-10 minutes
  5. let it sit to cool down, enjoy
