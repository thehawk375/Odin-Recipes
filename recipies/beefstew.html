Beef Stew

Home made beef stew

Ingredients

  • some form of stew meat
  • carrotts
  • onion
  • potatoes
  • beef broth
  1. Chop up vegetables
  2. cube and brown the meat
  3. add everything to slow cooker
  4. add seasonings to slow cooker
  5. cook on low heat for 8 hours, or high for 4 hours
  6. serve and enjoy!
