Italian Sausage

Baked Italian sausage with onions and peppers

Ingredients

  • Italian Sausage
  • Onion
  • Green Pepper
  • salt, pepper, olive oil

Steps

  1. Chop Onions and Peppers
  2. Mix veggies into a bowl
  3. Add some olive oil, salt, and pepper to bowl and mix up veggies
  4. layer veggies into oven pan, add sausage on top
  5. Cook at 400F for 30 minutes, turn sausages half way through
  6. Let them cool down for 10 minutes and then enjoy!
